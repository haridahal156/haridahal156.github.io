Hari Dahal

Department of Mathematical Sciences href="https://www.rpi.edu/"

He has interned at Applied Materials as both a Machine Learning Engineer and Data Scientist, applying advanced models in real-world industrial settings. His academic background includes an MSc and BSc in Applied Mathematics, maintaining a near-perfect GPA throughout.

Hari has received awards including the Joaquin B. Diaz Prize and the SIAM Travel Award, and has published on arXiv with ongoing contributions in distributed optimal transport. He is also an experienced teaching assistant and student leader in the SIAM RPI chapter.
